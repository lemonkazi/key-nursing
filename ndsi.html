National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS)

Our NDIS services are designed to provide comprehensive support to individuals with complex disability needs. We offer a range of services under the NDIS framework, including:
  • Disability Accommodation (SIL & STA): We provide Supported Independent Living (SIL) and Short Term Accommodation (STA) options for our clients, ensuring comfortable and safe living spaces.
  • Household Tasks: Our support workers assist with household tasks to maintain a clean and safe living environment.
  • Respite Care: We offer respite care services to provide relief to both clients and their families.
  • Social Support: Our team promotes social interaction and companionship, fostering a sense of community.
  • Assistance with Meals: We assist clients with meal consumption, ensuring their nutritional needs are met.
  • Medication Support: Our nurses provide accurate administration of medications and monitor their effects.
  • Complex Bowel Management: Tailored solutions for clients requiring assistance with bowel care.
  • Urinary Catheter Care: Expert management of catheters to ensure client comfort and health
  • PEG Feeding: Skilled management of percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy (PEG) feeding.
  • Behaviour Support: We provide behavior support services to assist clients in managing challenging behaviors.
  • Staff Training & Development: We offer comprehensive training and development for support workers to ensure they are well-prepared to meet the needs of our clients.
